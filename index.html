Smart Canteen Online Ordering Website

Designed and developed an online food ordering website for college canteen using Design Thinking methodology. The platform allows students to browse menu items, place orders online, and reduce waiting time in queues. The system improves canteen efficiency, minimizes crowding, and enhances user convenience. The project focused on user-centered design, problem identification, prototyping, and solution implementation.

Water Quality Sensor System

Developed a Water Quality Monitoring System for Chemistry project that measures the quality of drinking water using sensor-based analysis. The device checks parameters such as turbidity, pH level, and water purity to determine whether the water is safe for consumption. The system provides real-time output indicating safe or unsafe drinking conditions. This project demonstrates the integration of science, electronics, and technology for real-world health applications.
